Minesweeper

How to Play

The goal of Minesweeper is to uncover all of the tiles whilst avoiding the bombs. Simple!

  1. Click on a tile
  2. If the tile was hiding a bomb then it's Game Over
  3. If the tile is clear, it will show a number indicating how many bombs surround that particular tile
  4. Win the game by revealing all tiles that do not contain bombs

Hint: Use the tile numbers to work out where the bomb locations are and place a flag on them (by right clicking or selecting the flag and then clicking). You can then use flagged bombs to clear more tiles.
